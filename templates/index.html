Deployment of a Secure Ride-Sharing Decentralized-Application using Smart Contracts on Ethereum Blockchain

The concept of ride sharing has gained widespread acceptance and is in use by many people across the world. To maintain a secure database of customer details is a cardinal functionality of an organization. Blockchain is known for its trusted and tamperproof security. This article talks about the various advantages of implementing blockchain technology in the ride-sharing industry. This paper aims to develop a blockchain-based decentralized application that will allow users to avail ride-sharing services without any intermediaries. The smart contract is deployed in the Ethereum Blockchain. Each transaction in Ethereum is signed by a cryptographically signed contract. The fee is paid as Ether. Aside from payment, Ethereum also acts as a pricing instrument for running decentralized applications. A smart contract is a cryptographic bundle that processes and stores information on the Ethereum blockchain. Its output can only be accessed by the public if certain conditions are met. A prototype of the final result is developed as a Decentralized Application on the Ethereum blockchain.
